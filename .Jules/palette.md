## 2024-05-15 - Missing ARIA labels on dynamic icon buttons
**Learning:** Stories, notifications, and other dynamic features often use icon-only buttons (like emoji ✕ or SVG hearts) without `aria-label` attributes, making them inaccessible for screen readers.
**Action:** Always add descriptive `aria-label`s to icon-only buttons, especially those using emojis or SVG shapes without textual context.
